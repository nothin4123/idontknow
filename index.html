History of Artificial Intelligence

\n

Welcome to the History of AI website. Here you will learn about the key milestones and breakthroughs that have shaped the field of artificial intelligence.

\n

Key Milestones

\n
    \n
  • 1950: Alan Turing proposes the Turing Test.
    • \n
  • 1956: The term "Artificial Intelligence" is coined at the Dartmouth Conference.
    • \n
  • 1980: The rise of Expert Systems in business applications.
    • \n
  • 1997: IBM's Deep Blue defeats world chess champion Gary Kasparov.
    • \n
  • 2012: Breakthroughs in deep learning lead to advances in computer vision.
    • \n
  • 2020: Continued advancements in natural language processing.
    • \n

Future of AI

\n

The future of AI is bright with potential. Innovations in areas such as machine learning, robotics, and ethical AI will lead to significant advancements in various fields.
